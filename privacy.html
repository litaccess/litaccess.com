Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

LitAccess ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit litaccess.com or use our tools and services.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, access LitAccess tools, or contact us
  • Name — collected through account registration or contact forms
  • Account and usage information — including credit balances and tool usage related to your LitAccess account

We do not collect payment information directly. Payments are securely processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe and are subject to their privacy policies.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain LitAccess tools and user accounts
  • Process credit purchases and manage account balances
  • Send occasional updates, product announcements, and literacy resources
  • Improve our tools and services
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

LitAccess may use cookies or local storage to maintain login sessions, remember user preferences, and improve site functionality.

4. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate LitAccess:

  • Firebase — provides authentication, hosting, and database infrastructure. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — securely processes payments for credit purchases. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — routes API requests securely. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — powers AI passage generation and screener analysis features. Prompts and outputs are processed through Anthropic's API. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — serves fonts used on this site. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's privacy

LitAccess is a service directed at parents, educators, and language specialists — not at children directly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Information about a child's learning needs shared through our tutoring inquiry form is provided by a parent or guardian and is used solely to respond to that inquiry.

6. Data retention

We retain form submission data for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain our records. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at hello@litaccess.com.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of email communications at any time by replying to any email with "unsubscribe"

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@litaccess.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely and never exposed in our public code. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@litaccess.com.
